Cheese Onion Omlette

An omelette is the easiest thing to cook. Make it special with onion and parmesan and garnish with parsley. This can be your go-to breakfast on busy mornings since this is super quick and easy to cook!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Oil/ butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Onion, finely chopped
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Heat some butter in a pan. You can use oil too.
  2. Add some onions and let them caramelize (turn brownish).
  3. Whisk the egg in another bowl. Do not over beat the egg. Last thing you want is the egg to be fluffing up because the omelette has to be thin and for that it should not be over beaten.
  4. Add some salt, cheese and herbs to it.
  5. Normally you just fold the egg with the fillings of your choice but here the cheese and herbs are added to the egg batter itself.
  6. Now in another pan heat some butter/oil. It should be of a good amount otherwise the egg will stick to the pan.
  7. Pour the egg mix and spread it evenly. Add the caramelized onions to it. Top the with some more cheese.
  8. Fold one half of the egg over other and serve.
